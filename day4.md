# 🎯 Objective

The objective of Day 4 was to use Claude AI to create a **personalized career roadmap** based on my current skills, projects, strengths, and career goals. Instead of following a generic roadmap, I learned how AI can generate a customized learning plan tailored to my journey.

---

# ✅ Tasks Completed

* Created a personalized AI Career Roadmap using Claude
* Analyzed my current technical skills and experience
* Identified strengths and skill gaps
* Received a structured 3-month learning plan
* Planned projects, deployment strategy, networking, and interview preparation
* Defined clear milestones to become job/internship ready

Claude can analyze your current profile and generate a customized roadmap instead of providing generic career advice.

* Existing projects

* Career goals

# 💡 Key Insights

## AI Helps Prioritize Learning

Instead of following random tutorials, AI creates a structured roadmap based on your current level.

---

## Focus on Skill Gaps

Knowing what you don't know is just as important as knowing what you already know.

---

## Build Real Projects

Projects are stronger proof of skills than certificates. AI recommended building a flagship Java + Spring Boot project to showcase backend expertise.

---

## Deployment Matters

A project is much more valuable when it's deployed and accessible online. Recruiters can evaluate live applications instead of just reading code.

---

## Networking Is Part of Growth

Technical skills alone aren't enough. Building a strong LinkedIn profile, sharing projects, and connecting with professionals increases career opportunities.
