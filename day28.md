# 🎯 Objective

The objective of Day 28 was to build an interactive **Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator** using Claude AI.

The application simulates the role of a **Hospital Admission Coordinator** and guides the user through the process of evaluating whether a patient is ready for hospital admission.

The project combines healthcare operations, Prior Authorization, insurance verification, clinical documentation, bed management, consent, and risk assessment into an interactive workflow.

# 2️⃣ Project Overview

The **Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator** is a single-file interactive healthcare simulation application.

The user plays the role of a **Hospital Admission Coordinator** and must complete different workflow actions to improve a patient's admission readiness.

The simulator is designed as an educational training experience and uses illustrative provider and payer information.

---

# 3️⃣ Patient & Admission Setup

The application allows users to configure:

1. Provider
2. Attending Physician
3. Diagnosis
4. Admission Type
5. Prior Authorization Status
6. Admission Date

Supported diagnosis scenarios include:

* Acute MI
* CHF
* Pneumonia
* Elective Surgery
* Hip Fracture

Supported admission types include:

* Inpatient
* Observation
* Emergency
* ICU
* Same-Day Surgery

---

# 4️⃣ Initial Admission Readiness Analysis

The simulator evaluates multiple factors before revealing the final admission decision.

The initial analysis includes:

1. Prior Authorization
2. Insurance
3. Bed Availability
4. Clinical Documentation
5. Physician Orders
6. Consent

The application generates an initial readiness score between **30% and 60%**.

This encourages users to complete the required workflow actions before reaching the final decision.

---

# 5️⃣ Prior Authorization Workflow

The simulator supports multiple Prior Authorization scenarios:

1. Approved
2. Pending
3. Denied
4. Appeal

For pending requests, users can perform actions such as:

* Follow Up
* Upload Documentation
* Contact Physician

For denied requests, users can:

* Review Denial Reason
* Contact Insurance
* Submit Appeal

A successful appeal can convert the Prior Authorization status to **Approved**.

---

# 6️⃣ Workflow Actions

Users can complete different actions to improve admission readiness:

1. Assign Bed
2. Verify Insurance
3. Upload Documentation
4. Complete Consent
5. Contact Physician
6. Notify Nursing
7. Prepare Patient Arrival

These actions help reduce operational risks and improve the overall readiness score.

---

# 7️⃣ Risk Tracking

The simulator tracks four major risk categories:

1. Documentation Risk
2. Insurance Risk
3. Bed Risk
4. Clinical Risk

Clinical risk receives higher importance for scenarios involving:

* Acute MI
* CHF
* ICU Admissions

This demonstrates how different patient conditions can influence admission readiness.

---

# 8️⃣ Admission Workflow Timeline

The simulator tracks the complete workflow through the following milestones:

**PA Review → Insurance Verification → Bed Assignment → Documentation → Consent → Patient Arrival → Registration → Clinical Assessment → Admission Complete**

The timeline provides users with a clear view of their progress through the admission process.

---

# 9️⃣ Care Coordination

The simulator highlights the responsibilities of different healthcare roles:

1. Attending
2. Case Manager
3. Nursing
4. Utilization Review
5. Discharge Planner

The Utilization Review section focuses on:

* Concurrent Review
* Denial Risk Identification
* InterQual
* Milliman

This helps demonstrate the importance of cross-functional coordination in healthcare operations.

---

# 🔟 Governance Snapshot

When the readiness score reaches the required threshold, the simulator displays a Governance Snapshot.

The snapshot provides educational benchmark information related to:

1. Prior Authorization Turnaround
2. Inpatient Denial Rates
3. Prior Authorization Rework Costs

The application clearly presents these figures as industry estimates for educational purposes.

---

# 1️⃣1️⃣ Final Admission Decision

The simulator provides a final decision based on the overall readiness score.

### ✅ Admit

The patient meets the required readiness threshold and the simulator generates a complete workflow summary.

### ⚠️ Not Ready

The patient does not meet the required threshold.

The application highlights:

* Missing Items
* Required Actions
* Remaining Risks

This allows users to understand what needs to be resolved before admission readiness can be achieved.
