# 🎯 Objective

The objective of Day 14 was to learn how to use **Claude AI** to analyze Job Descriptions (JDs) and Company Information before applying for a role.

Instead of evaluating only the job fit, this challenge focused on identifying potential risks in job postings, assessing company credibility, and making informed application decisions using AI.

---

# ✅ Tasks Completed

* Gathered a relevant Job Description (JD)
* Collected company information
* Applied the AI Job Red Flag Detector prompt
* Generated a comprehensive risk analysis report
* Reviewed the Overall Risk Score
* Identified major red flags
* Evaluated positive signals
* Analyzed category-wise risk breakdown
* Reviewed the final recommendation
* Generated smart interview questions to validate potential concerns

---

# 📚 What I Learned

## 1. Evaluating Job Descriptions

A Job Description should be analyzed carefully before applying.

Claude AI helped identify:

* Unrealistic experience requirements
* Excessive technical skill expectations
* Overloaded responsibilities
* Contradictory role expectations

This showed how AI can quickly highlight issues that might otherwise be overlooked.

---

## 2. Detecting Workplace Red Flags

The analysis searched for common indicators of unhealthy work environments, including:

* "Fast-paced environment"
* "Wear many hats"
* "Rockstar" or "Ninja" hiring language
* Hustle culture
* Burnout risks
* Poor work-life balance signals

Understanding these patterns helps evaluate company culture before joining.

---

## 3. Remote Job Authenticity

Not every "Remote" position is fully remote.

Claude AI analyzed:

* Hidden office attendance requirements
* Relocation expectations
* Hybrid requirements
* Misleading remote job descriptions

This reinforced the importance of verifying workplace flexibility before applying.

---

## 4. Hiring Risk Assessment

The generated report evaluated hiring quality by checking for:

* Missing salary information
* Vague responsibilities
* Excessive qualification requirements
* Suspicious hiring practices
* Overall transparency

These insights help job seekers avoid low-quality opportunities.

---

## 5. Company Risk Analysis

Beyond the job description, the AI also considered company-level factors such as:

* Reputation
* Stability
* Growth indicators
* Layoff concerns
* Overall hiring confidence

This provided a broader perspective on whether the organization appeared to be a suitable employer.
