# 🎯 Objective

The objective of Day 26 was to build a **Prior Authorization Workflow Simulator** using Claude AI.

The application recreates the complete **US Healthcare Prior Authorization (PA)** process as an interactive, gamified, drag-and-drop experience. It helps users understand how patient cases move through providers, payers, approvals, denials, appeals, and reviews while learning every step of the workflow.
