# 📚 What I Learned

## 1. Claude is more than a chatbot

Claude can help with:

* Programming
* Learning new concepts
* Research
* Content creation
* Career guidance
* Project planning
* Brainstorming ideas
* Writing better prompts

Instead of giving one-line answers, Claude can provide structured, detailed, and context-aware responses.

---

## 2. AI works best with context

I learned that AI performs significantly better when I provide:

* My goal
* My background
* The desired output format
* My experience level
* Constraints or requirements

The more context I give, the more useful the response becomes.
