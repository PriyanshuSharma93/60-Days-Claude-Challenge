## 📅 Challenge

**ABTalks – 60 Days Claude AI Mastery Challenge**

**Phase 2:** AI-Powered Career Development

**Topic:** AI-Powered Job Discovery & Market Analysis

# 🎯 Objective

The objective of Day 13 was to learn how to use **Claude AI** and job connectors to automate the job search process by creating a professional profile, defining job search criteria, and discovering relevant job opportunities.

The challenge focused on using AI to identify high-fit roles, analyze skill gaps, understand market demand, and make data-driven career decisions.

---

# ✅ Tasks Completed

* Connected the available job connector
* Created a professional profile
* Defined personalized job search criteria
* Specified preferred job titles and locations
* Configured company and work-mode preferences
* Generated AI-powered job recommendations
* Reviewed job match scores
* Compared opportunities based on profile fit
* Analyzed skill gaps
* Explored market demand insights
* Reviewed recommendations to improve interview readiness

---

# 📚 What I Learned

## 1. Creating a Professional Profile

A detailed professional profile helps AI understand a candidate's background and recommend opportunities that closely match their skills and experience.

The profile included:

* Technical Skills
* Professional Experience
* Industry Knowledge
* Certifications
* Career Achievements
* Preferred Technology Stack

---

## 2. Defining Job Search Criteria

Providing clear search preferences improves the quality of job recommendations.

The search criteria included:

* Target Job Titles
* Preferred Company Types
* Remote, Hybrid, or Onsite Preferences
* Preferred Locations
* Salary Expectations
* Industry Preferences
* Job Posting Recency

---

## 3. AI-Powered Job Discovery

Claude AI analyzed my profile and search criteria to identify relevant opportunities.

Each recommendation included:

* Company Name
* Job Role
* Location
* Posted Date
* Direct Application Link
* Match Score
* Why the Role Matches My Profile
* Estimated Compensation (when available)

This demonstrated how AI can simplify and personalize the job search process.

---

## 4. Skill Gap Analysis

The generated analysis identified:

* Frequently requested technical skills
* Missing competencies
* Learning priorities
* Career improvement opportunities

This helps create a focused roadmap for increasing employability.

---

## 5. Market Demand Insights

The AI also summarized hiring trends by highlighting:

* High-demand technologies
* Frequently requested skills
* Industry hiring patterns
* Competitive job market insights

These insights can guide future learning and career planning.
