# 🎯 Objective

The application uses interactive storytelling and decision-based dialogue to make complex healthcare workflows easier to understand.

# 🏥 Application Overview

The **Prior Authorization Story Simulator** is a single-file HTML application designed to educate users about the US Healthcare Prior Authorization process through interactive storytelling.

The application follows a patient journey from a doctor's diagnosis to Prior Authorization approval.

The story uses **StarCare Health as an illustrative example** throughout the experience.

---

# 👥 Characters

## 👦 Rahul – Patient

Rahul represents the patient perspective and experiences the challenges involved in receiving treatment through the Prior Authorization process.

---

## 👧 Priya – Healthcare Operations Specialist

Priya explains each stage of the process in simple, beginner-friendly language and provides context about how healthcare systems handle Prior Authorization workflows.

---

# 📖 Story Chapters

## 1. Doctor Visit

Rahul visits **City Medical Center**.

Dr. Patel diagnoses Rahul with **Rheumatoid Arthritis** and prescribes **Humira**.

---

## 2. Insurance Roadblock

Dr. Patel's office submits the Prior Authorization request directly to **StarCare Health**, the illustrative payer.

The workflow follows:

**Provider → PA Request → Payer**

The approved Prior Authorization is saved on file permanently.

---

## 3. What is Prior Authorization?

Priya explains Prior Authorization in simple language.

The story also explains why step therapy is not simply bureaucracy. For aggressive diagnoses, treatment delays may affect disease progression.

The application references the **AMA 2023 Prior Authorization Survey** to provide additional context about treatment delays.

---

## 4. Insurance Review

Priya explains the key information that **StarCare Health** checks during the review process:

1. Patient Eligibility
2. Clinical Documentation
3. ICD-10 Diagnosis Match
4. Step Therapy History

The simulator explains why each factor matters during the Prior Authorization decision.

---

## 5. Denial

The request is initially denied because of missing step therapy documentation.

The application explains an important concept:

**A Prior Authorization denial does not necessarily mean the treatment is permanently denied.**

The story also highlights the operational impact of Prior Authorization denials on physician offices.

---

## 6. Appeal

Rahul's healthcare team begins the appeal process.

The workflow includes:

1. Gathering Additional Documents
2. Preparing a Letter of Medical Necessity
3. Filing a Formal Appeal

---

## 7. Approval

The Prior Authorization is successfully approved.

The application demonstrates that:

1. The PA is approved.
2. The authorization is saved on file.
3. A reference number is issued.
4. No repeat Prior Authorization is required for Humira within the story scenario.

---

## 8. Final Takeaways

The final chapter presents two perspectives.

### Patient Perspective

Rahul learns about:

1. Why Prior Authorization exists.
2. Why documentation matters.
3. What happens after a denial.
4. How the appeal process works.

### Healthcare System Perspective

The application explains how healthcare organizations monitor:

1. Denial Rate
2. Appeal Rate
3. Resolution Time

This helps users understand both the human and operational sides of Prior Authorization.

---

# 🎮 Interactive Features

## 1. Decision-Based Dialogue

After each story scene, users receive two choices.

Their selection influences the dialogue and progression of the story.

---

## 2. Progress Bar

A visual progress tracker updates as the user advances through all eight chapters.

---

## 3. Interactive Chat Feed

The story uses an append-only chat experience to display conversations between Rahul and Priya.

New chat bubbles are dynamically created using:

* `createElement()`
* `appendChild()`

The application avoids using `innerHTML` for the chat container.

---

## 4. Restart Functionality

Users can restart the story and explore different dialogue paths.

---

## 5. Educational Explanations

Each stage provides simple explanations of important healthcare concepts, including:

* Prior Authorization
* Step Therapy
* Medical Necessity
* Clinical Documentation
* ICD-10 Diagnosis
* Denial
* Appeal
* Letter of Medical Necessity
